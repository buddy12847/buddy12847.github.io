Garfunko Media

Garfunko Media Ltd. is a Garfunkian media conglomerate. Garfunko Media is the parent company of GAME NEWS, GAME STUDIO, and GAME TECH, as well as other companies in the Garfunkian entertainment market.

Garfunko Media was founded as "Garfunko Family Media" on C15-GY3 by Ian Dains, Zachary Dawson, and Nicholas Qualls as a merger between Garfunko Family News and newly created companies specializing in entertainment.

Garfunko Media is known for operating the Holy Temple of Garfunkia website, which it runs at no cost to the Garfunkian government, as well as for its ownership of GAME NEWS.
