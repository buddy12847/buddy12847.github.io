Royal Bank of Garfunkia

Founded L31-GY4 by Ian Dains, the Royal Bank of Garfunkia is the treasury of Garfunkia. As opposed to most contemporary banks, the Royal Bank is a full-reserve bank meaning the Royal Bank does not lend assets given to it. The Royal Bank does offer loans but it finances these loans through investments by businesses such as Garfunko Media, as well as through investments by private individuals. The Royal Bank accepts GC, VHS tapes, audio cassette tapes, gold, and silver. The Royal Bank of Garfunkia currently holds 33 VHS Tapes of varying length.

Ian Dains currently has the most assets stored in the Royal Bank, with 18 of his VHS tapes being held at the Royal Bank. The government of Garfunkia keeps 4 VHS tapes at the Royal Bank and Garfunko Media keeps 7 tapes at the Royal Bank. Garfunko Media has invested 4 VHS tapes into the Royal Bank to be used by the bank to finance loans.
